Kings of the Ptolemaic Era

Cleopatra VII

Last queen of Egypt, famous for her intelligence and power.

Ptolemy I Soter

Founder of the Ptolemaic dynasty, a general under Alexander the Great.

Ptolemy II Philadelphus

Known for patronage of science and expansion of the Library of Alexandria.

Ptolemy III Euergetes

Known for military strength and cultural achievements in Alexandria.
