The Most Important Landmarks of the Ptolemaic Era

Library of Alexandria

One of the most famous libraries in history, symbolizing knowledge and culture.

Philae Temple

Located in Aswan, dedicated to goddess Isis and known for its beauty.

Pharos Lighthouse

One of the Seven Wonders of the Ancient World guiding ships safely.

Dendera Temple

Famous for its astronomical ceiling and colorful carvings.

Ptolemais Hermiou

A Greek-style theatre used for performances and gatherings.

Pompey's Pillar

A massive ancient column and one of Alexandria's landmarks.
