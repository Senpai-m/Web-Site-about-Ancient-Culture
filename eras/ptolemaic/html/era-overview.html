Ancient Egypt Project

Ptolemy I Soter was a general under Alexander the Great and started the Ptolemaic dynasty in 305 BC. Capital Alexandria was the center of Greek culture, science, and a major hub for global trade. The Ptolemaic Kingdom was a powerful Hellenistic state. It began when Ptolemy I declared himself Pharaoh and ended with the death of Queen Cleopatra VII in 30 BC when Egypt became a Roman province. Mixing Greek and Egyptian cultures. Building the Great Library of Alexandria. Construction of the Lighthouse of Alexandria (Pharos).
