Coptic Heritage

The Copts are one of the oldest Christian communities in Egypt, with a history that dates back to the first century AD. Christianity was introduced to Egypt by Saint Mark, who is considered the founder of the Coptic Orthodox Church.

Over the centuries, the Copts developed a rich cultural and spiritual heritage that blends ancient Egyptian traditions with Christian beliefs. Despite facing many historical challenges, they have preserved their unique language, art, and religious practices.

Today, the Coptic community continues to play an important role in Egypt’s cultural and religious identity.
